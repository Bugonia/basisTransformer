  containing only references and the reproducibility checklist;
- supplementary material can be submitted separately.

## Hard Sprint Replan

The earlier 30-day sprint is now too relaxed. AAAI-27 gives:

- 12 days to abstract deadline from 2026-07-09;
- 19 days to full paper deadline from 2026-07-09;
- 22 days to supplementary/code deadline from 2026-07-09.

## Minimum Viable AAAI Submission

By 2026-07-21:

- title;
- abstract;
- author-safe anonymized framing;
- core claim frozen;
- at least preliminary tables for existing controlled experiments.

By 2026-07-28:

- 7-page main paper in AAAI format;
- all main tables and figures;
- open-model basis inventory;
- at least one pretrained-model logit attribution or causal write ablation;
- related work and limitations;
- clean references and checklist handling.

By 2026-07-31:

- code archive or anonymized repository snapshot;
- supplementary appendix with derivations, extra tables, and experiment
  commands;
- reproducibility material.
